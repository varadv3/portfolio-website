LIVING ROOM

Designing your living room involves a creative blend of style and functionality. Start by defining your design aesthetic and setting a mood with a color scheme.
Arrange furniture to encourage conversation and maintain a comfortable flow. Choose quality pieces that match the room's size.
Accessorize with decor that reflects your personality, and pay attention to lighting for ambiance.
Efficient storage solutions keep the space organized and functional.
