BACKYARD

Designing a backyard is the art of blending aesthetics with practicality.
Start by defining your outdoor design preferences, selecting a color scheme or natural elements that create a desired ambiance, and plan the layout for both comfort and visual appeal.
Choose outdoor furniture and landscaping that suits the space, adding personal touches with decor and accents. Thoughtful lighting and greenery contribute to a welcoming atmosphere.
